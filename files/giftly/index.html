Gift Card Disclosure

The Giftly Prepaid Gift is issued by Sunrise Banks N.A.

This is not a traditional gift card. The Giftly Prepaid Gift (or the "Giftly Gift Card") is a prepaid product that includes a suggestion for how the gifted funds could be spent.

Giftly Gift Cards can be sent electronically, printed at home, or sent in the mail. They are redeemed through the Giftly website.

Recipients choose how to receive the gifted funds, which they can spend at Kroger or anywhere else. They are not accepted by any third-party merchant and are not covered by the CARD Act.

Giftly Gift Cards never expire and there are no inactivity fees. Satisfaction is 100% guaranteed.

Why buy a gift card using Giftly?
